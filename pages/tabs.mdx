## Basic Tabs

- Required: `value` on `Tabs.Trigger` and `Tabs.Content`
- `tabSizing`: `'fit'` or `'full'` on `Tabs.List`

```jsx
<Tabs.Root defaultValue="tab3">
  <Tabs.List tabSizing="fit">
    <Tabs.Trigger value="tab1">One</Tabs.Trigger>
    <Tabs.Trigger value="tab2">Two</Tabs.Trigger>
    <Tabs.Trigger value="tab3">Three</Tabs.Trigger>
  </Tabs.List>
  <Tabs.Content value="tab1"><Box css={{py: '$3'}}><LoremIpsum/></Box></Tabs.Content>
  <Tabs.Content value="tab2"><Box css={{py: '$3'}}><LoremIpsum/></Box></Tabs.Content>
  <Tabs.Content value="tab3"><Box css={{py: '$3'}}><LoremIpsum/></Box></Tabs.Content>
</Tabs.Root>
```
